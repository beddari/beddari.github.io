#cfgmgmtcamp 2019 tuesday schedule tips

Feb 5, 2019
2 min read

Depending on your interest and investments in the various local pubs and bars last night you might be a bit tired. Relax, there are no choices to be made until after the keynote so be early to secure your front row seat!

11:20

Terraboard by @raphink

Oh Terraform, our favorite dull sharp knife!

Raphael is excellent at solving hard code problems in infra management and you won’t go wrong listening to him! I’ve been lucky to be able to do so for an inceasingly large number of years, and with this project he helps bring better UX to Terraform, something well worth a visit & your time!

12:20

Packer, Terrafrom and Jenkins by @simonmcc

My favourite current toolstack and someone doing the same as myself. I’ll join this echo chamber to feel better knowing others probably make the same mistakes I do.

Also Simon is a really great guy with lots of cross domain experience.

14:00

Keeping Track of Stateful Infrastructure by @HerrSpace

Running lots of cra.. heterogenous infra across multiple DCs with lots of users. And custom tooling to do it.

The topic seems overly wide in a “this is how we do it” sense … pretty hard to do an interesting talk if it has TOO much content. But, otoh, I’m happy they are sharing what they do and think that deserves support.

15:00

Hallway track

Recovering from all the content from the three last talks is going to take a while, so hallway track it is!

16:30

Hierarchical Config Data with Jerakia and Ansible

The talk you all have been waiting for? No? Not suprisingly I’m hoping you’ll join in when fellow Jerakia community member Henning Henkel talks about how he’s using Jerakia as a data provider for Ansible.

Also - if you are around tomorrow, join us for the first ever Jerakia Day to learn more and really try out the project.
